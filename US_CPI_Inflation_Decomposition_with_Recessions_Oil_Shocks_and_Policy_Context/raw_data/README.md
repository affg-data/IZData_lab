# Raw Data

This folder contains the original datasets downloaded from FRED.

Files:
- CPIAUCSL.csv – Consumer Price Index for All Urban Consumers
- CPIENGSL.csv – CPI: Energy
- CPILFESL.csv – Core CPI (Less Food and Energy)
- CPIUFDSL.csv – CPI: Food
- FEDFUNDS.csv – Effective Federal Funds Rate
- UNRATE.csv – Unemployment Rate
- USREC.csv – U.S. Recession Indicator

These files are kept in their original form and should not be modified.
